Plan Value
What the CHC Program Delivers for the Plan
HEDIS & Quality Measures
PHQ-4 baselines create behavioral health access and follow-up measure documentation. The 35–44 cohort distress flags (PHQ-4 avg 4.1) support FUH/FUM pathway identification — exactly the population at risk for avoidable utilization and quality measure failures.
SDoH Data & Population Intelligence
75% SDoH need rate and 261 PRAPARE completions deliver population-level social needs mapping that claims data cannot produce. Care access as the primary gap — 42% of resource connections are provider navigation — maps directly to HEDIS access and continuity measures.
Member Satisfaction
Dual-channel platform engagement (app + care team) for 825 members creates consistent, personalized touchpoints. 38.5% live-contact conversion demonstrates members value the interaction. Satisfaction metric formal assessment planned for Month 3+, with strong leading indicators from enrollment momentum.
Health Equity
CHC's diverse Texas Medicaid population faces layered barriers — food, housing, transportation, and care access. Pyx is actively connecting members to Baker Ripley, Memorial Assistance Ministries, Meals on Wheels, and Behavioral Health Services, addressing the social determinants driving disparate outcomes in the STAR program.

Presenter Notes — Value View
  • Lead with the conversion rate: 25.7% at 7 weeks is a headline number. The contractual target is 10%. This isn't marginal outperformance — it's structural.
  • Frame 261 PRAPARE completions strategically: More members have been screened than are enrolled. The social needs data is building faster than the roster — a deliberate design, not a gap.
  • Tie care access to quality: 42% of resource connections are provider navigation. HEDIS access measures — PCR, AWC, well-child visits — all depend on members having a connected provider. Pyx is actively closing that gap.
  • Month 3 is the inflection point: As the screening phase matures, resource navigation volume will accelerate. The SLA metrics currently in progress will move toward completion as the program shifts phases.
